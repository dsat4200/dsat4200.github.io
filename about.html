About The Author

Hey I'm Cammi and I'm the author of Joules + Igor. I got the idea for Joules + Igor when I watched Rick and Morty and thought, "what if Rick was a femcel"? Other influences include Inside Job, Dr Who, personal experiences, and various other games, manga, and anime.

As for this website, it was created as the final project for my web design class. The first iteration of this site was made for a slightly shittier comic about vampires, but I tore apart the already spaghetti-like source code to bring you Joules + Igor.

You can contact me at email or check out my shitty YouTube channel.

Also, follow me on Webtoon!
